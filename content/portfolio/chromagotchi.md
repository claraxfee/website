## About

Chromagotchi is a Chrome extension that gamifies mindful browsing. It gives you a customizable Tamagotchi-style virtual pet whose health rises when you spend time on tabs you've marked as important, and falls when you open distracting ones. Built collaboratively with three teammates for CIS 3500 Software Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania.

## Timeline and scope

Spring 2024. Designed, built, and shipped as a full group project over one semester, including a public landing page with dev blog posts and a demo video.

## Skills and technologies

React, JavaScript, Chrome Extensions API (background scripts, manifest v3), HTML/CSS, npm
